Our Wedding Timeline

This is how we have got all our rituals and events planned

  • Engagement

    November 21, 2020

    The fathers of both about to be married individuals vouch for the virtues of their child and make a formal wedding announcement of their children to the invited guests. The bride and the groom then exchange rings to solidify their engagement.

  • Haldi

    November 23, 2020

    In this ceremony a paste of haldi is applied on the bride and the groom's body before their wedding day. Haldi ceremony is directly connected with beautifying the bride and the groom for their big day. The colour yellow is also considered auspicious, and thus is said to ward off the evil.

  • Mehendi

    November 24, 2020

    Mehendi Ceremony is about applying Henna to the bride's and groom's hand and feet. This joyous celebration is often sees its complement with singing of traditional songs as well as playing of traditional music instruments like dholak.

  • Marriage

    November 25, 2020

    The main day around which all these events are organised. The bride and groom are seated next in front of a holy fire, as a priest recites various mantras from the Holy Scriptures. At the end of the ceremony, the bride and groom take seven vows in sanskrit, sealing the marriage forever.

  • Reception

    November 26, 2020

    This post wedding ceremony is basically a way of introducing bride to the groom's community. This event has no mandatory rituals to be followed, in fact the ceremony comes packed with dancing, music and a feast.
